Frequently Asked Questions

Is it Team Silverblue, Silverblue, or Fedora Silverblue ?

We chose the name Team Silverblue to refer to the overall project. The shorter Silverblue will be used for the OS that we are producing, but calling it Fedora Silverblue is perfectly fine as well.

Why does the Silverblue logo look like a leaf?

Our favorite choice for a project name was Silverleaf, but that sadly did not work out. We just couldn't quite let go fo the leaf.

You could also say that Silverblue is a new new leaf on Fedora's OSTree :-)

Is Silverblue another GNOME OS?

GNOME OS was a codename that was used by the upstream GNOME project for a while to refer to the idea of designing the entire user experience from boot through login to the desktop. Silverblue by contrast is an effort inside the Fedora project, and will be built with existing Fedora and Project Atomic technologies. One aspect that the two efforts have in common is the desire to deliver a user experience that is polished and coherent.
